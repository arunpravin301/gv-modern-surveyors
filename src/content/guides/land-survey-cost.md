One of the most common questions we get is: *"How much will it cost to survey my land?"*

The truth is, topographic surveying is not a one-size-fits-all service. The cost depends entirely on the size of the land, the complexity of the terrain, and the specific type of survey required.

Here is a transparent breakdown of how we price our services at GV Modern Surveyors:

## 1. Boundary Fixing (Standard Plots)
For standard residential plots (e.g., 30x40 or 40x60 feet) within city limits, the cost is usually a flat fee. This covers the mobilization of the team, the Total Station equipment, and the physical marking of the 4 corners.

## 2. Topographical Surveys (Acreage)
For larger tracts of land (1 acre and above), we charge on a per-acre basis. Topographical surveys require us to map not just the boundaries, but every tree, road, elevation change, and existing structure on the property.

## 3. Daily Rental Rates
For massive construction projects (like high-rise apartments or factories), builders often prefer to hire our team on a daily basis. A standard "Day Rate" includes:
- 1 Professional Surveyor
- 1 Assistant / Prism Man
- 1 Robotic Total Station
